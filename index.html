Abstract

Gentrification is the process of urban revitalization that often leads to the transformation of lower-income neighborhoods into more affluent areas. It involves the influx of wealthier residents, the renovation or construction of housing, and the subsequent displacement of existing residents. In this abstract, we will explore the concept of gentrification, its impacts, and its specific manifestation in Seattle.

Gentrification Explained

Seattle, like many other cities, has experienced significant gentrification in various neighborhoods. The process has resulted in the displacement of long-time residents, changes in the socioeconomic landscape, and the reshaping of the urban fabric. This section will delve into the specific dynamics of gentrification in Seattle, examining notable neighborhoods and the implications for local communities.
